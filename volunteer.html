Volunteering

It takes a community to create and offer a camp experience. Whether you have an hour, a day, or a week, there are many ways you can share your gifts and passions.

Volunteer levels

MENTORS are needed to invest a significant amount of time during the week of camp. Mentors will support the YMCA counselors, lead some activities, teach songs, "hang out" with the children during the day and through the night in blocks of time. Mentors will also help maintain contact with the children following camp and throughout the school year.

Other volunteers are needed to complete administrative tasks and gather needed resources prior to camp week.

Volunteer gatherings

There will be a series of gatherings in June and July for mentors to get to know each other, learn camp songs, and receive specific training.

Inquire about volunteering
